What is AI Hackathon?

AI Hackathon is a joint collaboration between Tata Motors Global Delivery Centre and Symbiosis Institute of Technology to nurture young minds in industry applications. First of its kind in the industry where we are looking for innovation in AI and NLP ( voice sentiment analysis) in English and Hindi.

AI Hackathon will be held in two rounds. The first round will be an online round where the problem statement will be shared over email the day Round 01 begins around mid August. Post evaluation, once shortlisted for Round 02, selected teams are requested to come over to SIT Pune campus for a 72 hour hackathon scheduled during the end of September. Details will be shared over email with shortlisted teams. So hurry up and register now !

FAQ

Who can participate?
Students of any college of India as well as startups less than two years old can participate.
How big the teams can be?
A team should contain 2-4 members including mentor.
Will there be travel reimbursements?
Travel expenses are to be borne by the teams themselves.
How much does it cost to register?
Registration is completely free.
What languages are we required to analyze?
  • For Round 01 - It's English and Hindi.
  • For Round 02 - It's a regional language of your choice.
What should I bring to the venue?
You need to carry your college ID (for college students), hard copy of startup registration certificate (for startups), laptop, phones, chargers and any other necessary equipment.
When will the registrations end?
The registrations will close at 11:59 pm on 10th August 2019. Shortlisted teams will be informed by August 16th via email.
Will accommodation and food be provided at SIT?
Accommodation and food for Round 2 will be provided by SIT.
What are the details of the cash prizes?
Cash prizes will be given away to winners of Round 02 (for two problem statements separately per team).
  • First Prize - Rs. 1,50,000*
  • Second prize - Rs. 1,00,000*
  • Third prize - Rs. 50,000*

*TDS as applicable

Can multiple teams register from the same college or startup?
Yes, there is no limit for teams registering for AI Hackathon.
