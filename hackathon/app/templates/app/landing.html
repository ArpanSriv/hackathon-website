What is AI Hackathon?

AI Hackathon is a joint collaboration between Tata Motors Global Deliver Centre and Symbiosis Institute of Technology to nurture young minds in industry applications. First of its kind in the industry where we are looking for innovation in AI and NLP ( sentiment analysis) in English and Hindi.
