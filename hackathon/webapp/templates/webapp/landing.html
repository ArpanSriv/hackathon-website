What is AI Hackathon?

AI Hackathon is a joint collaboration between Tata Motors Global Delivery Centre and Symbiosis Institute of Technology to nurture young minds in industry applications. First of its kind in the industry where we are looking for innovation in AI and NLP ( voice sentiment analysis) in English and Hindi.

AI Hackathon will be held in two rounds. The first round will be an online round where the problem statement will be shared over email the day Round 01 begins around mid August. Post evaluation, once shortlisted for Round 02, selected teams are requested to come over to SIT Pune campus for a 72 hour hackathon scheduled during the end of September. Details will be shared over email. So hurry up and register now !

FAQ

Who can attend?
Students of any college of India as well as startups less than two years old can attend.
How big the teams can be?
A team should contain 2-4 members including mentor. Selected teams would be invited to the Round 2 at SIT (Symbiosis Institute of Technology), Pune.
Will there be travel reimbursements?
Travel expenses are to be borne by the teams.
How much does it cost to register?
There is no registration fees.
What should I bring to the venue?
You need to carry your college ID (for college students), hard copy of startup registration certificate (for startups), laptop, phone, chargers and any other necessary equipment.
When will the registrations end?
The registrations will close on 11:59 pm on 9th August 2019. Shortlisted teams will be informed by August 16th through email.
Will accommadation and snacks/food be provided at the hackathon?
Accommodation and snacks/food will be provided by the SIT campus hostel.
